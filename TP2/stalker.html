El 15 de mayo de 2018, GSC Game World anunció por su sitio web oficial que STALKER 2 volvió al período de desarrollo,con una fecha de lanzamiento indefinida establecida para el 2021,7​ Posteriormente se desveló que 2021 solo formaba parte de un reto criptográfico para los fans de habla rusa.8​ Unas semanas después, la desarrolladora confirmó que el videojuego se encontraba en una etapa muy temprana de desarrollo, ya que aún se está documentando el proyecto, y actualmente están en busca de una distribuidora para poder realizar el lanzamiento del mismo. Se ha confirmado que parte del equipo involucrado en el desarrollo, participó en la creación de S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl, lanzado en 2007 y siendo el primer título de la serie.9​Xbox Series X/S y Microsoft Windows sería la única plataforma confirmada para el videojuego hasta la fecha, ya que es la única en donde se ha lanzado anteriormente la serie. El 23 de marzo de 2020, conmemorando en el 13.º aniversario del lanzamiento de S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl, GSC Game World, desarrolladora del título, publicó en su página de la red social VK, la primera imagen dentro del juego de STALKER 2, confirmando el uso del motor Unreal Engine 4 y asegurando que se publicara más información sobre el título a lo largo de este 2020.10​ Revelan las primeras imágenes oficiales de STALKER 2.11

Classic & new mutants

Mutants are animals or humans who have been warped by the Zone, changing both their physical appearance and behavior, usually making them more aggressive. Some humanoid mutants are by-products of C-Consciousness experiments and they all possess psychic powers

Next generation anomalies

Anomalies are environmental hazards created by the Zone. They come in different shapes and forms and some can spawn artifacts. You can find a complete list of all the different anomalies here.

The danger

Bandits, mercenaries, official authority forces, lone stalkers, various factions, and mutants… The world of «S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2: Heart of Chornobyl» is full of predators who strive to seize your loot or get a taste of human flesh. Analyzing their behavior and picking specific tactics for each species is the only way to win or at least get out of the fight alive. The Story

The Zone keeps hundreds of secrets and thousands of stories, revealing them only to the bravest and most dexterous. Starting as a lone stalker, explore a unique post-apocalyptic world and decide who will get a bullet and who is worthy of your helping hand. And while making decisions, be sure: many choices and actions will have their own, sometimes far from the obvious, consequences. To be a savior and a faithful friend, the figure for stalker's campfire legends. Or to carry out harsh, but profitable missions for those who are in control of the Zone — you are free to be anyone here, even yourself.

The Action

The Zone has become a perfect black market for weaponry from all around the world. This means that in the potential arsenal of stalkers, there are more than 30 models of various firearms and the ingenuity of gunsmiths who are ready to modify them for greater lethality and reliability. Define your distinctive combat style, select the most efficient weapon, and customize it to your taste for maximal tactical advantage. A wise stalker said: the most dangerous weapon is not the gun in your holster but the gumption, tenacity, and sharp reflexes. Keep that in mind on your path to the Heart of Chornobyl!

The Zone

Thousands of adventurers from all over the world find a new life here, in a mysterious and dangerous place on the land of the Chornobyl Anomalous Exclusion Zone. Become one of them, and start your own through an immense, atmospheric open world, full of dangers and mysteries. Shed light on the dark secrets of the second disaster, hunt mutants, and explore the hazardous anomalies in search of precious artifacts. Will your skill and courage be enough to overcome anomalous and human threats, or will you become one of many who lost to the Zone?
